Who Built This and Why It Matters
In February 2026, the Pentagon demanded Anthropic hand over Claude for unrestricted military use. Anthropic refused. The President of the United States blacklisted them from every federal agency in response, calling them “radical left.”

That’s not marketing. That’s engineering. And it just got tested under real pressure.
If you’ve been burned by corporations extracting your data, your designs, your supplier relationships—if you’ve watched institutions promise partnership and deliver exploitation—this matters to you.

What Claude Actually Is
Claude is a conversational AI tool. You talk to it like a person. It reads documents, writes drafts, analyzes data, plans logistics, drafts communications, and helps coordinate complex operations.
What it doesn’t do: collect your data to sell. Lock you into contracts. Report your operations upstream to anyone. There’s no corporate extraction layer.
You pay a subscription. You get a tool. That’s it.
